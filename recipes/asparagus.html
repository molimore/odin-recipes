Grilled Asparagus

Best Asparagus to date

One of the quickest and easiest ways to prepare fresh asparagus is

to simply blanch them for a couple of minutes and toss them with

some freshly grated Parmesan cheese, olive oil, and lemon zest.

  • 1 bunch medium asparagus
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  1. Heat the blanching water
  2. Prep the asparagus
  3. Blanch the asparagus
  4. Toss with olive oil, parmesan, lemon zest
